# SEE Sign Lookup

A mobile-friendly web app for looking up **Signing Exact English (SEE)** signs.

## [Launch App →](https://toyesm.github.io/SEE-helper/)

Open the link above on your phone to use it on the go.

## Features

- **3,600+ signs** from the Signing Exact English dictionary with hand-drawn illustrations
- **Word search** with autocomplete suggestions
- **Sentence mode** — type a full sentence and step through each word's sign
- **Voice search** — tap the mic and speak a word or sentence
- **A–Z quick jump** and special sections (Numbers, Alphabet, Contractions, Affixes)
- **Dark mode** support
- **Works offline** once loaded — all images are served locally

## How to Use

1. Open the app on your phone
2. Type a word in the search bar (or tap the mic for voice input)
3. The sign illustration appears immediately
4. In Sentence mode, type a full sentence and tap through each word
5. Tap any word shown on the page to search for it directly
